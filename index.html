Welcome to SnapPortal

SnapPortal is a civic issue reporting platform that empowers citizens to report problems in their community — such as potholes, water leaks, broken streetlights, and more — by simply snapping a photo and uploading it.

About SnapPortal

SnapPortal is more than just a website — it's a civic empowerment initiative developed by a team of engineering students passionate about creating real-world impact. Our aim is to give a voice to the often unheard and overlooked citizens by offering them a simple, effective way to report local civic issues.

Whether it's a pothole damaging vehicles, a broken streetlight causing safety concerns, or uncollected waste posing health risks — SnapPortal enables individuals to upload photographic evidence of these problems directly to a centralized platform where they can be seen and acte upon.
